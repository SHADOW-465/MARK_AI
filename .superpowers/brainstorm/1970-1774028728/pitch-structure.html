Section 3: Pitch Document Structure

A one-page institutional pitch for school principals + a deeper sales deck. Two artifacts, two purposes.

ARTIFACT 1 — One-Page Principal Brief
MARK AI
India's first platform that grades answer sheets and helps students recover — built for CBSE schools
The Problem
Teachers grade 40–60 answer sheets per class. By the time feedback reaches students, it's too late to act on it. Students repeat the same mistakes in every exam.
What MARK AI Does
AI grades handwritten answer sheets in minutes, identifies each student's exact error type (concept / calculation / keyword), and generates a personalized recovery plan — automatically.
What Your School Gets
✓ Teachers save 6–8 hours per exam cycle
✓ Principal dashboard: class-wide performance at a glance
✓ NEP 2020 / PARAKH-aligned continuous assessment
✓ Student AI study guide — no extra tutoring needed
✓ Parent portal with real progress data
Why Now
CBSE is rolling out On-Screen Marking (OSM) for Class 12 from 2026. MARK AI is designed for exactly this workflow. Schools that adopt early set the standard.
Pilot Offer
Free for one term. No setup fee. We configure it for your marking schemes. You evaluate the results.
Format: Printed A4 / PDF. Leave-behind after principal meeting.
